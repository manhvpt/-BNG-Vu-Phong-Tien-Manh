THE MAGIC WORLD OF ZODIAC SIGNS

The project “The Guardians of Zodiac Signs” is tailor-made from my passion for astrology.

 The project also contains stylized and symbolic details which are inspired by Vietnamese traditional culture.

IDEA & SKETCHES

Project "The Guardians of Zodiac Signs" was launched in November 2019 and completed in January 16th 2020. On the first month of making the project, I collected all astrological knowledge i had before, researched more additional infomations for first ideas preparation. The idea is based on Horoscope's circle and RGB color wheel is color base for each signs.

Two months left, i forcused on making the characters, positions, characteristics and added some Viet- nam's symbolic and stylized details such as ao dai, mourning veil, conical hat.
